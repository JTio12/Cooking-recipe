Omelette Recipe

Easy to follow omelette recipe

Ingredients
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) of milk
  • 1 dice tomato
  • 1/2 cup of chop basil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of butter
    • Cooking Instructions
  • Crack the eggs into a bowl, add the milk, tomato,basil and season with salt and pepper and beat the mixture with a fork or whick.
  • Heat a medium sized pan over a medium heat and add the butter, allowing it to melt.
  • Add the egg mixture to the pan and as it begins to cook. use a spatula to push cooked parts from the edge to the centre of the pan.
  • Wait for few minutes until it cooks completely according to your likeness
